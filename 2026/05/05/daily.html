🥇 A blueprint for using AI to strengthen democracy

MIT Technology Review published a framework for AI-driven democratic reform — arguing that AI’s ability to process vast information flows could counter misinformation and improve civic participation.

Why it matters: The proposal reframes AI not as a threat to democracy but as a potential tool to modernize governance in an era of information overload.

🏷️ Policy Democracy Governance

AI is ‘creating an enormous number of jobs,’ says Nvidia’s Jensen Huang

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang pushed back against AI job-loss fears — claiming the technology is generating more roles than it eliminates.

Why it matters: Huang’s stance contrasts with widespread worker anxiety, framing AI as a net job creator rather than a disruptor.

🏷️ Labor Economy Nvidia

AI data centers head for the ocean

Companies are exploring underwater data centers to cool AI infrastructure — while also highlighting how to replace Siri with free local models.

Why it matters: Offshore data centers could cut cooling costs and energy use, but raise new environmental and logistical challenges.
