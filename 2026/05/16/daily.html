🥇 Researchers train AI model that hits near-full performance with just 12.5% of its experts

New modular MoE model achieves 95% accuracy while activating only one-eighth of its parameters — The approach uses dynamic routing to select the most relevant experts for each input, cutting compute costs without sacrificing output quality.

Why it matters: If scalable, this could make large models far cheaper to run, lowering barriers for startups and researchers.

AI made a tiny slice of Silicon Valley filthy rich and left the rest wondering why they bother

A handful of AI founders and investors captured most of the wealth, while engineers and support staff see little benefit — The gap between equity-rich insiders and salaried workers is widening, with some questioning whether the AI gold rush is sustainable for anyone outside the inner circle.

Why it matters: The concentration of wealth could slow innovation if talent leaves for fairer opportunities—or if disillusionment spreads beyond the Bay Area.

Google says GEO and AEO are a myth and traditional SEO is all you need for AI search

Google’s latest guidance downplays “generative engine optimization” and “AI engine optimization,” insisting classic SEO still works — The company argues that high-quality content and standard ranking signals remain the best way to surface in AI-powered search results.

Why it matters: If Google’s right, marketers can skip the hype and focus on fundamentals—saving time and money.
