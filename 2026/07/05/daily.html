🥇 AI search agents don’t fail at searching—they fail at asking the right questions

New research shows AI search agents trip on ambiguity, not execution — When users ask vague or open-ended questions, agents often misinterpret intent rather than botch the search itself.

Why it matters: Better query refinement could cut hallucinations by half in agentic search.
