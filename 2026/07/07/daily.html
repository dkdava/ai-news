🥇 British Space Startup Launches Longevity Lab Into Orbit

A UK space startup sent a microgravity lab to orbit — The lab will study proteins linked to Alzheimer’s and cancer, feeding data to AI models that predict disease behavior.

Why it matters: Microgravity could reveal protein structures impossible to observe on Earth, accelerating drug discovery.

The first American autonomous ground vehicles are fighting in Ukraine

Why it matters: Real-world deployment could accelerate AI-driven warfare, raising ethical and strategic questions.
