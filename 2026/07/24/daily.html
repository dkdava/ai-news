🥇 How AI guardrails are impeding the work of offensive cybersecurity researchers

Cybersecurity researchers say OpenAI and Anthropic’s guardrails hinder vulnerability discovery — Teams building exploit tools hit walls when models refuse to generate or analyze malicious code.

Why it matters: The same safety layers meant to stop bad actors are also slowing down the good guys trying to find flaws first.

At AI Summit, South Korea outlines its AI future with NVIDIA and partners

South Korea’s president and business leaders met with NVIDIA at the AI Summit to map out the country’s AI strategy — The partnership focuses on infrastructure, talent, and industry adoption.

Why it matters: A rare public roadmap from a government tying national AI goals directly to a single vendor’s tech stack.

Some kids will never think AI is cool

Kids as young as nine are calling AI “disgusting” and “artificial idiot,” rejecting the hype — Interviews reveal skepticism, boredom, and even fear of AI tools.

Why it matters: Early generational pushback could reshape how schools and parents introduce AI literacy.

Black Forest Labs teaches video AI to run robots

Black Forest Labs’ video models now train robotic systems, while also offering AI-powered video editing for non-experts — The tools aim to automate both physical tasks and creative workflows.
