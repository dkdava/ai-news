🥇 AI is more likely than humans to form biases when hiring

Researchers found AI hiring tools develop stronger biases than human recruiters — LLMs trained on real-world hiring data amplify existing discrimination patterns, even when instructed to be neutral.

Why it matters: Companies rushing to automate hiring may unknowingly lock in systemic bias at scale.

Moonshot pauses new Kimi K3 subscriptions after GPU demand maxes out in 48 hours

Moonshot hit capacity limits and froze new signups for its Kimi K3 model — The surge followed a pricing update and feature expansion.

Why it matters: China’s AI infrastructure is straining under demand, signaling rapid adoption but also hardware bottlenecks.

Anthropic’s Fable survives the subscription axe
