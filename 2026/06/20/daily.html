🥇 ChatGPT keeps creeping toward becoming your AI personal assistant with new scheduled task controls

OpenAI rolled out scheduled task controls in ChatGPT — letting users set reminders, recurring actions, and time-based triggers directly in the chat interface.

Why it matters: This moves ChatGPT closer to a true productivity layer, not just a reactive chatbot.

Data2Story turns a CSV file into a verified interactive news article using seven AI agents

Data2Story automates data journalism — a pipeline of seven specialized AI agents cleans, analyzes, fact-checks, and writes a publishable story from raw CSV input.

Why it matters: Could lower the barrier for data-driven reporting, but raises questions about editorial oversight.

OpenAI tripled revenue to $5.7 billion in Q1 but burned through $3.7 billion to get there

OpenAI’s Q1 revenue hit $5.7B, up 3x year-over-year — but the company spent $3.7B in the same period, mostly on compute and talent.

Why it matters: Growth is explosive, but the burn rate suggests scaling AI is still a capital-intensive game.
