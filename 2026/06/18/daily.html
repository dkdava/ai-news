🥇 How to turn off AI in your Google Docs

Google Docs now lets users disable Gemini’s “write with AI” prompts — A new settings toggle removes the persistent pop-ups for those who prefer manual writing.

Why it matters: The move signals growing pressure on tech giants to offer opt-outs as AI features become default in productivity tools.

Google’s Gemini co-lead Noam Shazeer joins OpenAI

Noam Shazeer, a key architect of Google’s Gemini, has left for OpenAI — His departure follows a two-year return to Google after a stint at Character.AI.

Why it matters: Shazeer’s move underscores OpenAI’s continued pull on top AI talent, even from rivals with deep pockets.

🏷️ Talent OpenAI Google

France advances Europe’s AI future with NVIDIA

France is scaling its AI infrastructure using NVIDIA hardware — The country’s push includes new AI factories, national compute capacity, and open frontier models.

Why it matters: France’s investment could shift Europe’s AI center of gravity away from traditional hubs like London or Berlin.
