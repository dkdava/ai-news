🥇 SpaceX signs $920 million per month deal with Google for 110,000 Nvidia AI chips ahead of IPO

SpaceX has secured a $920 million monthly contract with Google for 110,000 Nvidia AI chips — the deal precedes SpaceX’s anticipated IPO later this year.

Why it matters: This is one of the largest AI hardware deals to date, signaling SpaceX’s push to dominate both space and terrestrial AI infrastructure.

OpenAI and the Trump administration are negotiating a government stake in the AI startup

OpenAI is in talks with the Trump administration to secure a government stake — a move that could reshape the company’s governance and alignment with U.S. policy.

Why it matters: A government stake would mark a major shift in OpenAI’s independence, potentially influencing its research priorities and global partnerships.

Qwen3.7-Plus is Alibaba’s bid to turn multimodal AI into a full-blown autonomous agent

Alibaba’s Qwen3.7-Plus aims to evolve multimodal AI into a fully autonomous agent — combining vision, language, and tool use in a single model.

Why it matters: If successful, this could position Alibaba as a leader in agentic AI, challenging Western dominance in the space.
